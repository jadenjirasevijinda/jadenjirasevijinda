Editing in DaVinci Resolve
Video· Beginner
›
1
Import footage — open the Media page, drag your files into the Media Pool.
2
Create a timeline — drag clips from the pool to the Edit page timeline.
3
Cut and trim — use the Blade tool (B) to cut, drag edges to trim clips.
4
Color grade — jump to the Color page, apply a LUT, then dial in your nodes.
5
Export — go to Deliver, choose YouTube preset, hit Add to Render Queue then Render All.

02
Recording Audio
Audio· Beginner
›
1
Connect your mic — XLR into Channel 1 on the Rodecaster Pro. Enable phantom power for condenser mics.
2
Set gain — speak at normal volume and aim for peaks around -12 to -6 dB on the meter.
3
Enable APHEX processing — Aural Exciter and Big Bottom add warmth and clarity to your voice.
4
Record via USB — select the Rodecaster as your audio input in DaVinci Resolve or your DAW.
5
Monitor with headphones — always track with cans on so you catch any issues live.
